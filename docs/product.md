# CookCart Product Document

## Product Name

CookCart

## Product Type

B2B SaaS platform for grocery stores with customer-facing web and mobile apps.

## One-Sentence Pitch

CookCart helps grocery stores sell customizable meal kits where customers choose meals, receive exact ingredient quantities, remove ingredients they already have, and access recipe and nutrition details through web, mobile, and QR-enabled packaging.

## Problem

Customers often do not know what to cook, buy too many ingredients, waste food, and struggle to match meals with their goals.

Grocery stores sell individual products, but customers think in meals.

## Solution

CookCart turns grocery products into meal-based shopping experiences.

Customers choose a meal, customize the ingredient kit, and order only what they need.

## Core User Flow

1. Customer creates an account.
2. Customer adds basic profile details.
3. Customer adds pantry ingredients manually.
4. Customer browses meals.
5. Customer opens a meal.
6. App shows exact required ingredients.
7. Customer removes ingredients they already have.
8. Customer adds the meal kit to cart.
9. Customer places an order.
10. Admin receives the order.
11. Admin prepares the meal kit.
12. Customer scans QR codes on ingredient packages.

## MVP Customer Features

- Register and login
- View meals
- View meal details
- Manage pantry
- Customize meal kits
- Add to cart
- Place simulated orders
- View order history

## MVP Admin Features

- Manage ingredients
- Manage meals
- Assign ingredients to meals
- Manage orders
- Update order status
